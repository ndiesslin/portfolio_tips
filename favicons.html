DESKTOP & MOBILE FAVICONS

Prerequisites: Web Standards, FTP

Desktop Favicons

Desktop favicons are the little icons next to the URL as well as your page title in the browser tabs. These little images can help to reinforce your brand identity and help your users distinguish between tabs. Websites built to standards include this detail.

Mobile Favicons or "Touch Icons"

Mobile favicons will be displayed if a person saves your website to their home screen on a mobile device. If you don’t have a mobile favicon defined then the phone OS automatically designates a screenshot of your webpage to be displayed as the icon. Websites built to standards include this detail.

For a more in-depth look at mobile favicons, check out this link http://mathiasbynens.be/notes/touch-icons.
